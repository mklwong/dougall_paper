Model Setup

Mathematical formula: as per dQSSA paper.

All Akt components are modelled with dQSSA kinetics while remaining kinase activity (PIP3 formation, TSC2 and mTORC1 phosphorylation) are modelled with mass action kinetics (which assumes negligible volumes of enzyme-subtrate complex relative to dissociated species of both enzyme and substrate).
